_Added: 2026-02-26 | Target audience: 25-30 year old guys, golf + gambling_

## Brand Direction

**Vibe:** Vegas meets Augusta. High-stakes golf weekend energy.  
Think: clean luxury casino + modern golf + lads weekend.  
NOT: old-school country club. NOT: cartoonish gambling.

## Color Palette

Primary palette:
- **Deep Black:** `#0A0A0A` ‚Äî main backgrounds
- **Emerald Green:** `#00C853` ‚Äî primary actions, CTAs, success states, money
- **Gold:** `#FFD700` ‚Äî champions, trophies, premium elements, wins
- **Warm White:** `#F5F5F5` ‚Äî primary text
- **Slate Gray:** `#1C1C1E` ‚Äî card backgrounds, secondary surfaces
- **Muted Gray:** `#3A3A3C` ‚Äî borders, dividers

Accent:
- **Red:** `#FF3B30` ‚Äî danger, losses, delete actions
- **Amber:** `#FF9500` ‚Äî warnings, side bets, in-progress

NO MORE: purple gradients, blue gradients, generic dark teal

## Typography

- **Headings:** Bold, uppercase or title case ‚Äî authoritative
- **Numbers/Stats:** Tabular numbers, prominent sizing
- **Tournament name:** "THE GAMBLER" in wide-tracked all-caps everywhere it appears
- Use existing font stack but lean into weight contrast (900 weight for hero text, 400 for body)

## Design Language

### Cards
- Black backgrounds (`#0A0A0A` or `#1C1C1E`)
- Thin gold or green accent borders on featured/active cards
- Subtle inner glow on hover (green for positive, gold for champions)
- Sharp corners OR very slightly rounded (4px max) ‚Äî no bubbly UI

### Buttons
- Primary: Solid emerald green `#00C853`, black text, bold
- Secondary: Transparent with green border
- Danger: Solid red
- Money/payment actions: Gold gradient with black text
- All caps text on buttons

- Silver `#C0C0C0` for 2nd
- Bronze `#CD7F32` for 3rd
- Green numbers for scores under par, red for over
- Monospace/tabular numbers for all scores

## Animation / Motion

Keep it subtle but impactful:
- Score saves: brief green flash
- Champion reveal: gold shimmer/pulse
- Leaderboard rank change: slide animation
- Payment success: confetti or chip animation
- Loading states: skeleton screens (dark themed)

## Mobile Considerations

- Large tap targets (min 48px)
- Scorecard entry: Big number buttons, easy to tap drunk
- Shot tracker: Full-screen with massive "LOG SHOT" button
